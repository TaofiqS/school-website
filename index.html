ABOUT US



The Freshman Caucus lead by Mufei Yu and Johanna Li organizes exciting events and fundraisers to help raise money for and support our Stuyvesant. We strive to create a fun and engaging environment for our student body, as we understand that the intense rigor and pressure of transitioning to Freshman year. It can be overwhelming but we work to help our peers foster valuable friendships and connections through the variety of activities we organize. From the creative events & fundraisers to our various sources of academic opportunities, we work to lend a hand to the fellow Freshman and improve the overall school environment. As the Freshman Caucus, we're thankful for the chance to support Stuyvesant freshman and eager to lead toward a better future.

-Executive Department
